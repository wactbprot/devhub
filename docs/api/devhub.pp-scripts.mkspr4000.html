calq-fm3

(calq-fm3 task)

Pre calculation of the flow q setpoints sp1 and sp2 to reach the target pressure p in ts seconds. Pressures are in mbar.

Example:

(ctrl-str
   (cut-fs
      (calq {:p 27, :dev -1.0, :mq 0.018, :ts 20.0, :glim 0.7, :slim 1.0, :md 1.0})
   {:fs1 1.0 :fs2 100.0}))
