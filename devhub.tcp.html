query

(query {conf :tcp} {cmds :Value, i :EOT, n :NL, :as task})

Sends the cmds given by the :Value to a raw tcp socket with the specified host and port.

Example:

(def c (c/config))
(def t {:Port 5025 :Host "e75496" :Value ["room()\n"] :Wait 10 :Repeat 2})
;; prologix mks670
(def t {:Port 1234 :Host "192.168.98.204" :Wait 10 :Repeat 10
:Value ["++addr 2
++auto 1
++eot_char 10
:meas:func
"]})
(query c t)
